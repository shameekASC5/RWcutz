About Ronald

Why He Started

All across boarding schools in the Northeastern United States young men struggle to find barbers comparable to the ones they treasure back home. One day a young lad decided to help his brethern and began a journey to learn how to cut hair. What began with youtube videos and terrible haircuts evolved into a business known today as RW Cutz. Ronald is now the primary barber for young men across his school's New England campus.

Haircuts for All

An critical part of RW Cutz mission is to provide haircuts to all, no matter the hair texture. Mr.Wang has experience with all different hair types and styles and enjoys accomodating the requests of all his customers.
